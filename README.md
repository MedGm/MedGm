## About Me

I am a **Software and Intelligent Systems Engineering student** with a strong focus on **Machine Learning, MLOps, and AI-driven applications**.  
My passion lies in bridging **research, engineering, and real-world impact** by building tools, deploying intelligent systems, and experimenting with **next-generation AI workflows**.

I thrive at the intersection of:
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**, from classical ML to LLMs and deep learning
- **MLOps / LLMOps**, scalable training, deployment, monitoring, and model lifecycle management
- **Full-Stack & System Engineering**, building platforms that merge AI with usable products
- **Software Development & Interactive AI**, exploring how AI can adapt and learn in real-time environments

Currently, I am expanding my knowledge of **LLMs, reinforcement learning, and cloud-native deployment pipelines**, while contributing projects that make AI **accessible, transparent, and efficient**.
